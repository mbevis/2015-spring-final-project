Day of the Dead Girl
    2012
    Montana Bevis
    Acrylic on Canvas

This project was created when I was in the midst of Community College, and was an attempt at doing a portrait other than myself. While at the time self portraits were quite useful for improving my skill, it was a bit exhausting to paint yourself repeatedly. So, when the class was given absolute freedom, I took advantage of the fact that Day of the Dead had recently flown by and painted a woman with Catrina Girl make-up. Using an open source facial reference found on deviant art, and a found image of a tulip, I painted this character and gave her a very minimal design.
